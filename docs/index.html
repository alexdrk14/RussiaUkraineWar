Overview
We initiated a data collection gathering from Twitter regarding the Russo-Ukrainian War, on 24 February, 2022. The main goal is to analyze the main trends and topics discussed in this online discourse, watch the tendencies of users, the suspension of potential malicious entities, the sentiment of the text, the hate speech or any propaganda that may be visible through OSNs.
